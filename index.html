Hi, I'm Adhishree

I'm a senior undergraduate student at Indian Institute of Technology-Bombay, studying Chemical Engineering. At IIT-B, I was part of an awesome team who manufactured Autonomous Underwater Vehicles every year for the annual Robosub competition representing India. I worked on the fifth iteration of the vehicle called Matsya 5.0. I also undertook several Mentoring and Teaching activities. In junior year I worked as Undergraduate Research Assistant with Prof. Abhijit Majumder. During senior year Summer I worked as a ULIP intern with Unilever Bangalore team at the end of which I was offered a pre-placement interview.

A lot of my projects involve simulation and producing videos, most mesmerising of which are compiled on my Youtube Channel.

You can get me interested in virtually anything by answering 2 questions- 1. Practical Utility of the endeavour? 2. Mathematics of it? in that order :P

My research interests include Aerosols Science, Air Quality, Environmental Fluid Mechanics and Atmospheric Chemistry.

Well thumbed Nonfiction books include Seven Habits of Highly Effective People and Six Thinking Hats through which I hope to master being efficient and have more time to pursue my hobbies. You can find other books I have read and plan to read in the Reading Section.

My hobbies include trekking and nature trailing and picture-blogging about it later. Another quirky activity I spend a lot of time on is learning different languages. I like to think this stems from a curiosity about different cultures. The to-learn list currently includes Japanese and French. Sometimes I write poems which very soon seem embarrassing to me and other compiled musings from various places on the web which you can find here.

I start my day as well as end it with Yoga. I also play Table Tennis and Badminton and Swim occasionally. I also love to memorise Marathi Poems and Songs and recite them off the top of my head.
